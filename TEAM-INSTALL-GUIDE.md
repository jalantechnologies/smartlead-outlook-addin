## Installing the Smartlead Outlook Add-in

### Step 1: Get the Manifest File

Your admin will send you a file called `manifest.xml`. Save it to your computer.

### Step 2: Open Outlook Web

1. Go to [outlook.office.com](https://outlook.office.com) or [outlook.office365.com](https://outlook.office365.com)
2. Sign in with your work email

### Step 3: Open Any Email

Click on any email message to open it (required for next step)

### Step 4: Access Add-ins Menu

**Look for one of these options in your toolbar:**

- Click the `...` (three dots) button, then select **Get Add-ins**
- OR click **Apps** button â†’ **Get Add-ins**
- OR find the Add-ins icon in the ribbon

### Step 5: Go to My Add-ins

1. In the Add-ins window, click **My add-ins** in the left sidebar
2. Scroll down to the bottom

### Step 6: Upload the Manifest

1. Click **+ Add a custom add-in**
2. Select **Add from file**
3. Click **Browse** and select the `manifest.xml` file
4. Click **Upload**
5. Read the warning and click **Install**

### Step 7: Wait for Installation

- A message will appear: "Smartlead Integration has been added"
- Close the Add-ins window

### Step 8: Find the Add-in Button

1. Open or refresh any email
2. Look for the **"Add to Smartlead"** button in the toolbar
3. It might be in the `...` menu if your screen is small

## First Time Setup

### Get Your Smartlead API Key

1. Go to [app.smartlead.ai](https://app.smartlead.ai)
2. Sign in to your account
3. Click your profile â†’ **Settings**
4. Go to **API** section
5. Copy your API key

### Configure the Add-in

1. Open any email in Outlook
2. Click the **"Add to Smartlead"** button
3. The add-in will open on the right side
4. Click **Settings**
5. Paste your Smartlead API key
6. Click **Save API Key**

**Done!** Your add-in is now configured.

## How to Use

### Adding an Email Contact to Smartlead

1. Open any email from a candidate or contact
2. Click the **"Add to Smartlead"** button
3. The add-in shows the sender's email and name
4. Select a campaign from the dropdown
5. Click **"Add to Campaign"**
6. You'll see a success message!

### The contact is now in your Smartlead campaign!

## Troubleshooting

### I don't see the "Add to Smartlead" button

**Try these solutions:**

1. **Refresh Outlook**
   - Press F5 or refresh the page
   - Wait 1-2 minutes

2. **Check if add-in is installed**
   - Go to Get Add-ins â†’ My add-ins
   - Look for "Smartlead Integration"

3. **Try a different email**
   - Some special emails might not show add-in buttons
   - Open a regular inbox email

4. **Sign out and back in**
   - Click your profile â†’ Sign out
   - Sign back in to Outlook

### "Failed to load campaigns" error

**Solutions:**

1. **Check your API key**
   - Click Settings
   - Verify your API key is correct
   - Copy it again from Smartlead
   - Save it

2. **Check Smartlead account**
   - Make sure you have at least one campaign
   - Verify your account is active

### "Failed to add lead" error

**Solutions:**

1. Make sure you selected a campaign
2. Check your internet connection
3. Verify the email address is valid
4. Try again in a few seconds

### Add-in shows blank screen

**Solutions:**

1. Close and reopen the add-in
2. Refresh Outlook (F5)
3. Try in a different browser
4. Clear browser cache
5. Contact your admin

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do I need to install anything?**
A: No software installation needed! It's a web add-in that works in your browser.

**Q: Will this work on my phone?**
A: Currently designed for Outlook Web (desktop browsers). Mobile support may vary.

**Q: Can I use this in Outlook Desktop app?**
A: It depends on your setup. Works best in Outlook Web.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes! The add-in only sends data to Smartlead, which you already use.

**Q: Do I need admin permissions?**
A: No admin permissions needed to sideload the add-in.

**Q: Can I remove the add-in?**
A: Yes! Go to Get Add-ins â†’ My add-ins â†’ Click the three dots on Smartlead Integration â†’ Remove.

**Q: How do I update the add-in?**
A: Updates are automatic! Your admin updates the hosted files and you get changes automatically.

**Q: What happens to leads I add?**
A: They go directly into your selected Smartlead campaign, just like adding them manually.

**Q: Can I add the same email twice?**
A: Yes, but Smartlead will update the existing lead instead of creating a duplicate.
